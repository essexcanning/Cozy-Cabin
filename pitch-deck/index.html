How It Works

The Core Game Loop

👫 For Couples

  • Receive personalised Love Tasks (chores, affirmations, surprises)
  • Complete tasks together to earn Cozy Coins
  • Spend coins to build & decorate your cabin
  • Explore your shared space in real-time multiplayer

🤖 The AI Bear

  • An autonomous NPC that lives in your cabin
  • Senses game state & relationship signals
  • Proactively walks over to offer coaching & nudges
  • Pushes insights into your Luffa chat thread

Track: Vibe Coding

Zero Manual Code. 100% AI.

Every single line of this project was generated by AI. The human role was Product Architect, steering the Google AI Studio Antigravity agent through high-level prompts.

Track: Creative AI

Magic Easel & Projector

🎨

Magic Easel

Turn text-based memories into persistent pixel-art paintings for your cabin walls using Gemini 3 Flash Image (Nano Banana 2).

"Our first holiday in Cornwall" → a framed painting on the wall.

📽️

Magic Projector

Generate 5-second lo-fi video loops from shared dreams using Veo, projected inside the virtual cabin space.

"A sunset beach with our dog" → plays on the cabin projector.
